Hi, I'm Ayan.

Six years ago, anxiety and panic disorder walked into my life uninvited — and nothing has been the same since. There hasn't been a single day in these six years where I felt completely free, completely myself. But somewhere in the middle of the hard days, the therapy sessions, the conversations with people I love, and the tiny victories that nobody else could see, I started to change. Not the disorder — me. I got stronger, more patient, more honest with myself than I ever thought I could be.

I'm not on the other side yet, but I'm walking toward it — and that matters more to me than I can put into words. This blog is my way of making sense of the journey, sharing what I've learned, and maybe making someone else feel a little less alone in theirs. If you're here, fighting your own quiet battles, I want you to know — you found the right place.
